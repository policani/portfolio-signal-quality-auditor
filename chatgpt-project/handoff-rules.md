# Handoff Rules

## Handoff principles

Only pass records downstream when the receiving module can use them without inheriting hidden data-quality problems.

## To Portfolio Intake and Readiness Triage System

Pass:

- Raw demand
- Intake-needed records
- Shaping records
- Records missing business owner, sponsor, scope, or readiness

Do not pass:

- Confirmed active governed work unless it needs re-intake
- Duplicate candidates without a review note
- Records with invented or assumed fields

## To Portfolio Prioritization Scoring Agent

Pass:

- Records classified as ready for scoring
- Records with enough scope, sponsor, value logic, risk, dependency, size, and readiness context

Do not pass:

- Records missing sponsor or value logic
- Records with unresolved duplicate status
- Records whose KPI or status fields are too weak for fair comparison

## To Executive Portfolio Review Pack Builder

Pass:

- Reporting-risk summary
- Decision asks
- Confidence level
- Data-quality implications
- Recommended executive discussion topics

## To PMO Governance Operations Log

Pass:

- Cleanup actions
- Owners to confirm
- Decisions to chase
- Stale-status reviews
- Duplicate-demand review tasks
- KPI-definition follow-ups
