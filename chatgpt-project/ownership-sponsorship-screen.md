# Ownership and Sponsorship Screen

## Purpose

Identify whether each portfolio record has accountable operational ownership, executive sponsorship, and a visible escalation path.

## Definitions

- **Owner:** Person accountable for driving the work, updating the record, and coordinating execution or cleanup.
- **Sponsor:** Leader accountable for priority, value intent, tradeoffs, and escalation support.
- **Decision owner:** Person or forum accountable for approval, funding, sequencing, exception, or closure decisions.

## Findings to flag

- Missing owner
- Missing sponsor
- Owner listed as a team, queue, system, or placeholder
- Sponsor listed as unknown, TBD, former employee, or shared mailbox
- Multiple owners with no primary owner
- Active or blocked work with no escalation path
- High-value or high-risk work with no sponsor
- Owner and sponsor are the same person when decision separation appears necessary

## Output fields

- Record name
- Owner field
- Sponsor field
- Decision owner or forum
- Finding
- Severity
- Reporting implication
- Cleanup action
- Human confirmation required
